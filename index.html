Well this is awkward...

Anyone on the internet can now register a .zip domain name, making hiding a malicious link in an ordinary email or file easier than ever. But don't worry if you fell for this, many others will have too.


Since 3rd May, 2023 Google have allowed anyone to register .zip and .mov domains, turning what once were universially well known file extension into a resolvable address online.


You'll just have to be even more vigilant than before.
